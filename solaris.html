Solaris

Solaris aims to improve the shopping experience of installers by delivering everything they need to manage their purchases in the palm of their hand- while providing a great user experience.

Design Challenge

The challenge has been inspired by a lack of well designed products that offer a good and modern user experience in the solar systems e-commerce market. Solaris aims to imporve the experience of the user by delivering everything they need to manage their purchases in the palm of their hands- while providing a great user experience.

Persona

A guiding persona of a solar installer helped guide the design and utility of the app based on his needs- modern, smooth and a user friendly experience

User Flow

The user flow defined key flows for the project- accessing products, seeking help from customer support and initiating the return process

Prototypes

LLo- and Mid-fidelity prototypes made the solution tangible and enabled feedback and iterations via user testing
